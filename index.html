FAQ

How long will it take to receive my Jathakam?

We aim to deliver the completed Jathakam within 7 working days. Requests from donors are prioritised, and we aim to deliver them within 3 working days.

These timeframes are estimates rather than guaranteed delivery dates. Occasionally, high request volumes or the need to clarify submitted information may cause delays.

How will I receive the completed Jathakam?

Your completed Jathakam will be sent as a PDF attachment to the email address provided in the form.

Please check that your email address is entered correctly before submitting the form. We also recommend checking your spam or junk folder if you do not see the email in your inbox.

What information do I need to provide?

To prepare the Jathakam, we require the following details about the person for whom it is being created:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Exact time of birth
  • Place of birth

We will also require the requester's name, email address, and consent to use the submitted information for preparing and delivering the Jathakam.

Can I request a Jathakam for another person?

Yes, provided you have that person's consent to share their information and request a Jathakam on their behalf.

Users requesting the service may submit up to five Jathakam requests. This limit helps us prevent misuse and ensure that the free service remains available to genuine users. Donors may submit an unlimited number of requests.

What should I do if I entered incorrect information?

Please contact us through the form on our Contact page within 24 hours of submitting your request.

Clearly mention the incorrect information, the correct details, and enough information for us to identify your original submission.

Is the Jathakam service free?

Yes. The Jathakam service is available free of charge.

We also accept donations from users who wish to support the service. All donations are optional and non-refundable.

What is the purpose of making a donation?

Donations help us maintain and continue providing the service.

Requests from donors are given priority, with an estimated delivery time of 3 working days. Donors also receive priority support for questions or issues and may submit an unlimited number of Jathakam requests.

Please note that delivery times remain estimates and are not guaranteed.

Is AI used to prepare the Jathakam?

No. Artificial intelligence is not used to calculate, write, interpret, or generate any part of the Jathakam.

We believe that preserving the traditional process is important to respecting the sanctity of astrology and the beliefs associated with it.

How is my personal information stored and used?

The information you provide is used only to prepare and deliver the requested Jathakam, communicate with you about your request, and help prevent misuse of the service.

We will not use your information to send spam or unrelated marketing communications. Please read our Privacy Policy for complete information about how your data is handled and deleted.

What should I do if I do not receive my Jathakam or need help?

Please contact us using the form on our Contact page.

Select the most accurate reason for contacting us and provide enough information for us to understand the issue and identify the relevant submission. Before contacting us about a missing delivery, please also check your spam, junk, and promotions folders.
